DuaBiskutTelur — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

DuaBiskutTelur ("the app") lets you photograph a meal, uses AI to identify the food and estimate its nutrition, and grades the meal. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

Information we collect

  • Google account info (if you sign in): your name, email address, and profile picture, via Google Sign-In. We use this only to identify your account and personalize your history.
  • Meal photos you upload: sent to Google's Gemini API for food identification. A small thumbnail of each photo is stored with your history so you can review past meals; the original full-size photo is not retained after analysis.
  • Nutrition data: food names are looked up against the USDA FoodData Central database to estimate calories and macros.
  • Profile details you choose to enter: age, sex, weight, height, activity level, and daily calorie goal — used only to calculate your personal calorie budget.
  • Usage data derived from the above: your meal history, streaks, and achievement badges.

If you use the app as a guest (without signing in), none of your meal data is saved — it exists only in your browser session and is lost when you close or refresh the app.

How we use this information

Solely to provide the app's features: identifying food in your photos, calculating nutrition and calorie totals, tracking your history and progress, and calculating a personalized daily calorie budget. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication.
  • Google Gemini API — for meal photo analysis and feedback text.
  • USDA FoodData Central — for nutrition lookups.

Each of these providers processes the specific data described above under their own privacy policies.

Data storage and retention

Signed-in users' meal history, profile, and account data are stored on our server for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual meal entries at any time from the History page. To request deletion of your entire account and all associated data, contact us using the email below.

Children's privacy

DuaBiskutTelur is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
